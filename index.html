The Story

We were at a Hackathon and we wanted a simple way to exchange contacts and discuss with people at the event. There was nothing elegant out there. So we created Ripple.

We blogged and it got viral.

We learned Xcode along the way.

We did some beta and listened.

Why Ripple ?

For meetups and events

Meet like-minded people

Ask questions and learn

Destroy boredom
